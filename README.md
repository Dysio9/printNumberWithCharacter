Program shows the picked (5 digit) number as formated text. User can choose the type of format digit

For example, number 00099 and digit '$' should be shown like this:

```
$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$
$                                      $
$   $$$$   $$$$   $$$$   $$$$   $$$$   $
$  $$  $$ $$  $$ $$  $$ $$  $$ $$  $$  $
$  $$  $$ $$  $$ $$  $$ $$  $$ $$  $$  $
$  $$  $$ $$  $$ $$  $$  $$$$   $$$$   $
$  $$  $$ $$  $$ $$  $$   $$     $$    $
$   $$$$   $$$$   $$$$   $$     $$     $
$                                      $
$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$$
```
